User Management

To add new users to your organization:

  1. Go to Admin â†’ Users
  2. Click Add User
  3. Fill in the user's information
  4. Assign appropriate role and permissions
  5. Send invitation email
Users will receive an email invitation to join your organization.

Available Roles:
  • Admin: Full access to all features and settings
  • Manager: Can manage sales, customers, and team members
  • Sales Executive: Can manage leads and customers
  • Viewer: Read-only access to assigned areas
Sales & CRM

Effective lead management is crucial for sales success:

Lead Lifecycle:
  1. New Lead: Initial contact or inquiry
  2. Qualified: Lead meets your criteria
  3. Proposal: Quote or proposal sent
  4. Negotiation: Discussing terms and conditions
  5. Closed Won: Deal completed successfully
  6. Closed Lost: Deal not won

The sales pipeline helps you visualize and manage your sales process:

  • Track deals at each stage
  • Identify bottlenecks in your sales process
  • Forecast revenue based on pipeline data
  • Monitor team performance
